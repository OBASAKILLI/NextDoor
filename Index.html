Welcome to Nextdoor Homecare LLC

Nextdoor Homecare LLC is a leading home care agency committed to providing exceptional non-medical and medical services to individuals throughout the state of North Carolina. Our agency is founded on the principles of compassion, excellence, and a genuine commitment to enhancing the quality of life for those in need of both medical and non-medical personalized care.

As a trusted partner in care, we understand the importance of creating an environment where individuals can age gracefully, recover with dignity, and thrive in the comfort of their own homes. Our agency specializes in a comprehensive range of services, with a focus on nursing care, designed to meet the unique needs of each client we serve.

At Nextdoor Homecare LLC, we believe in the power of compassion, the pursuit of excellence, and the importance of upholding the highest standards of integrity. Whether you are a member of our dedicated team or a valued client and family, this commitment serves as a shared resource to ensure transparency, understanding, and adherence to the principles that guide our agency. Thank you for entrusting Nextdoor Homecare LLC with your care needs. Together, let us embark on a journey of compassion, respect, and excellence in home care.

Vision & Mission

🎯 Our Vision

At Nextdoor Homecare LLC, we envision a community where every individual, regardless of their health challenges, experiences the warmth of compassionate care in the comfort of their own home. Our vision is to be the premier home care agency in the State of North Carolina, setting the standard for excellence in non-medical and medical services, with a particular emphasis on nursing care.

🏥 Our Mission

The mission of Nextdoor Homecare LLC is to provide safe, comprehensive, and person-centered home care services in compliance with the North Carolina Home Care Licensure Rules (10A NCAC 13J) and other applicable state and federal regulations. We are committed to promoting dignity, independence, and quality of life for individuals served in their homes. Through the delivery of high-quality non-medical and medical services, including skilled nursing care, we develop and implement individualized plans of care based on assessed needs, ensuring continuity of care, client safety, and regulatory compliance.

Our Services

Comprehensive home care solutions tailored to meet your unique healthcare needs.

Skilled Nursing Care

Professional nursing services delivered by licensed and certified nurses, including wound care, medication management, and post-operative care in the comfort of your home.

Personal Care Assistance

Non-medical assistance with activities of daily living including bathing, grooming, dressing, and mobility support to help clients maintain independence.

Companion Care Services

Compassionate companionship services providing emotional support, conversation, and assistance with social activities to combat isolation and enhance quality of life.

Homemaker Services

Professional assistance with light housekeeping, meal preparation, laundry, and other domestic tasks to help clients maintain a safe and comfortable living environment.

Physical Therapy Support

Coordination with physical therapists and assistance with therapeutic exercises to support recovery, rehabilitation, and mobility improvement at home.

24/7 Care Availability

Round-the-clock availability for emergency support and care needs, ensuring our clients always have access to professional assistance when needed most.
